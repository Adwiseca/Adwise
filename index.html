FAQ

Common questions
answered honestly

Do I need any marketing experience?
None at all. Our AI handles strategy, targeting, and copywriting. You just answer a few questions about your business.
Where does my ad budget go?
Directly to Meta or Google — never through us. You maintain full control of your ad spend at all times.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see their first leads within 48–72 hours of launching. Full optimization takes 2–4 weeks as the AI learns your market.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with one click.
Is AdWise only for Canadian businesses?
Yes — we're built specifically for the Canadian market. Pricing is in CAD and our AI is trained on Canadian ad data.
How is AdWise different from hiring an agency?
Agencies charge $1,500–3,000/mo and lock you into contracts. AdWise costs from $149 CAD/mo and shows you everything in real time.
What if my ads don't perform?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the platform fee. If you don't see results, you don't pay.
Do I need a Facebook Business account?
We'll guide you through setting one up in 5 minutes if you don't have one. It's free and requires no technical knowledge.
